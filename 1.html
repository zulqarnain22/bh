FAQ Section

Is compounded tirzepatide the same as Zepbound?

Compounded tirzepatide contains the same active ingredient as brand-name Zepbound and Mounjaro. The difference is it's made by a compounding pharmacy rather than the original manufacturer. This is legal and FDA-regulated. Compounded versions exist because of ongoing shortages of the brand-name drugs.

How do these providers get such different prices?

Overhead, mostly. Bigger companies spend millions on advertising and have larger teams. Smaller operators keep costs down. The medication quality is comparable - price differences reflect business model, not product quality.
